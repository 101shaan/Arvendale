FromSoft-Inspired Elements

Bonfire System: Rest points called "Beacons" that restore health but respawn enemies
Souls Currency: "Essence" collected from defeated enemies, lost upon death but retrievable
Estus-like Healing: A refillable flask that replenishes at Beacons
Cryptic NPCs: Characters like "Patches the Unbroken" or "Siegward of Catarina" with hidden motives
Boss Naming Convention: "X, [Title]" (e.g., "Vordt, Guardian of the Frost Gate")

LOTR-Inspired Elements

Races: Humans, Elves (fading from the world), Dwarves (reclusive miners), Halflings (rarely seen)
Ancient Evil: A dark power awakening in the east, corrupting the land
Legendary Weapons: Items with rich histories like "Glamdring, the Foe-hammer"
Poetry and Songs: Found in books or sung by NPCs, revealing lore
Fellowship: Gathering allies throughout your journey who provide unique benefits

Combat System Refined

Stance-based: Choose aggressive, balanced, or defensive stance affecting attack/defense balance
Timing Windows: Text prompts with countdown for perfect parries/dodges
Enemy Weaknesses: Discover and exploit specific vulnerabilities (fire, silver, etc.)
Weapon Arts: Special abilities for each weapon type with cooldowns
Death Penalty: Loss of gathered Essence but chance to recover it
